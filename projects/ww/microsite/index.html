How it works

New Simple Start. Nail your first two weeks.

It's a two-week plan to start losing weight right away. This is our simplest plan ever to get you off to a great start. We even have Simple Start Webcasts† with tips and advice to help get you going strong.

After a great start, keep it going.

Eat the foods you love, and we'll show you how to make them part of a weight-loss program. Weight Watchers is flexible enough to fit your life — that's why it's a program you can stick with.

How do you do Weight Watchers? You choose.

You can decide how you want to do Weight Watchers: Lots of support and motivation through weekly meetings or follow the program entirely online with Weight Watchers Online.

† Included with a Weight Watchers Online or eTools subscription
